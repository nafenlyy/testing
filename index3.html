Situation of Sitio Bebe

residents of Sitio Bebe suffer from a variety of problems, including poor access to healthcare, troubles with their principal jobs, and problems with the local environment. They will need a variety of approaches to address their health issues and improve their general well-being, including access to clean water, the development of healthcare infrastructure, opportunities for job training, and specific actions to address respiratory problems and other health issues.


Factors that caused these problems are:

  1. Closing of mines and factories
  2. Lack of Access to Clean Water
  3. Lack of employees in training centers
  4. Pollution

How to Help:

A way to help this community is to have a fundraising event and fundraising events can help them give or have the money that is needed and to provide a good amount of service. With that, it can help spread awareness to the situation of Sitio of Bebe since multiple people can give donations, make news articles, and even create a social media campaign.
